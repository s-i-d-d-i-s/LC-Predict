Leetcode Rating Predictor

  • Leetcode takes around 5 days to update ratings !
  • Waiting for rating change can be boring :(
  • So here i present Leetcode Rating Predictor. Using this you can check your predicted ratings as soon as 30-60 minutes after the contest
Predict Ratings
  • Leetcode Rating Predictor needs manual efforts.
  • After every contest, i need to gather all data (using cloud CPUs because they are fast and im webscraping)
  • After getting all data, i run calculations for rating changes.
  • And then push the results to the frontend
  • I try my best to keep this service free, but small donations of around 0.5$ to 1$ can help me sustain this project longer
